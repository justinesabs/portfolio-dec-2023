My ReflectionReflection Page

Describe the department or functinal area and its methods and operation.

Maintaining the infrastructure performance, availability, and dependability of the business is mostly the responsibility of the SRE department. SREs use a combination of software engineering and system administration, working at the intersection of development and operations, to build software systems that are reliable and scalable.

As an Intern, how did the department benefit from you?

I provided a new perspective and a great sense of anticipation as an intern. To acquire knowledge, taking an active role in assignments and supporting my fellow interns on getting acquainted to tools and environment. My enthusiasm for exploring and understanding modern technology brought a feeling of creativity into the department.

Summarize how you spent the hours during your practicum including specific accomplishments

I committed my hours to a variety of things during my practicum, from gaining online credentials to mastering new technologies. Notable achievements include delving deeply into server configuration and creating docker images I made for docker containers. Furthermore, this is the first time I'vebuilt my first application network that could communicate with one another.

What have you learned from the experience in the company?

The internship offered a comprehensive educational opportunity. Using tools like Docker and Terraform, I became an expert in containerization and deployment and gained a thorough understanding of system reliability concepts. Being exposed to post-mortem analysis and incident response protocols provided insightful knowledge about continuous improvement.

How was your experience from the people in the company?

Acquiring knowledge from experienced SREs and multidisciplinary groups was an enriching encounter. The environment of learning promoted candid conversation, and the mentorship I was given was extremely important to my career growth. The group's eagerness to impart information encouraged a positive learning atmosphere.

The most memorable event during your practicum?

The instant I made it through the entrance written exam on my first try was indelible in my memory. And when I successfully completed a task assigned by one of our mentors was the moment I knew I would be a good fit in this line of work.

Recommendation you have for the company in order to facilitate practicum experience in the future.

In order to improve practicum experiences going forward, the organization might think about putting organized mentorship initiatives, making sure interns get regular feedback, and direction. Promoting participation in interdisciplinary projects can offer interns with a more comprehensive comprehension of the company's activities.

Your advice to those who will take their practicum in the near future.

I advise upcoming interns to approach issues with interest, look for chances to actively participate, and build trusting connections with coworkers. Don't be afraid to ask questions; the internship is about learning as much as it is about what you can do.

Conclusion

After completing my internship at Fligno Software Philippines, I can confidently say it was a rewarding and fulfilling experience. The company boasts a supportive work culture, with employees and mentors who are always willing to lend a helping hand, fostering an environment that encourages growth and development. Throughout my internship, I was exposed to a plethora of cutting-edge technologies and was involved in meaningful projects that challenged me to think critically and apply my skills. Fligno's commitment to excellence and innovation is truly inspiring, and the company consistently aims to stay ahead of the curve. Additionally, working with diverse teams provided me with the opportunity to expand my communication and collaboration skills, further enhancing my professional capabilities. Thank you Fligno Family <3
